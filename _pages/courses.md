## Courses

---

### 🌀 Fluid Mechanics (MECH 310)
**Overview:**  
Introduction to the fundamentals of fluid behavior, including statics, dynamics, and flow analysis.

**Topics include:**
- Fluid properties and statics  
- Conservation laws (mass, momentum, energy)  
- Laminar and turbulent flow  
- Applications in engineering systems  

---

### 🧬 Mathematical Modeling of Physiological Systems
**Overview:**  
Development and analysis of mathematical models to understand biological and physiological processes.

**Topics include:**
- Ordinary and partial differential equation (ODE/PDE) models  
- Mechanistic and deterministic models  
- Pharmacokinetics/pharmacodynamics (PK/PD)  
- Systems biology and feedback control  
- Data-driven modeling and simulation
